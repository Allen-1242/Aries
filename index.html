The Problem

AI agents in regulated domains have no proof they follow the rules.

Modern LLM-based agents are deployed across healthcare, legal, financial, and public benefit systems, domains where every decision has legal and human consequences.

Today's guardrails catch errors after the fact. Prompt engineering drifts. RAG pipelines hallucinate. There is no formal guarantee of compliance.

The result: organizations face liability, regulators have no audit trail, and the people these systems serve are exposed to silent, undetectable errors.

Aries provides proof, not promises.

We encode domain rules using formal knowledge representation. These rules are not heuristics. They are machine-verifiable logic.

Before any agent action reaches a user, our verification engine proves compliance against those rules. Not checks. Not filters. Mathematical proof.

The LLM plans. The deterministic executor acts. Nothing escapes verification.

How It Works

Three layers. One guarantee.

01 / Ingest

Your Rules, Formalized

Aries ingests your domain's regulatory and compliance requirements and converts them into a machine-readable rule set. No configuration by hand. Your existing policy documents become the source of truth.

02 / Plan

The Agent Proposes

Your AI agent generates a proposed action. It does not execute freely. Every output is treated as a candidate, held for verification before it reaches a user or downstream system.

03 / Verify

Proof Before Execution

Aries checks the proposed action against your rule set. If it passes, it executes. If it doesn't, it's blocked: silently, automatically, every time. No exceptions, no drift, no surprises.
